Project Description

Puja Samagri Online is an e-commerce platform specializing in religious and ceremonial items. It offers a variety of products including daily puja items, deity dresses, temple decor, and special puja kits.

The website features a user-friendly interface and provides nationwide delivery across India. Customers can find traditional products such as camphor scented wicks and deity photo frames, as well as modern puja accessories.

The story

The client had noticed a gap in the availability of traditional Indian religious items, especially for those living away from major cities. They wanted a platform where they could sell authentic puja items. Although they were selling their products on Amazon, the commission charges were too high, and their products were labeled as generic. After years of hard work, someone else claimed their product names. Despite complaints to Amazon, nothing changed. They decided to create their own website using WordPress, WooCommerce, and Divi so they could manage it themselves.

OUR APPROACH

First, I took input from the client about their product and asked for reference websites they had in mind. After gathering all the details, designer had designed the homepage, shop page, and inner pages. Once the design was finalized, we began implementing the website. It took around 1.5 months to complete the project. After numerous changes, the website was finalized and went live on the server.
